# Day 1 – Foundations of LLMs and RAG

This day covers the fundamental concepts needed to understand and build with Large Language Models and Retrieval-Augmented Generation systems.

## Modules

1. **Foundations of Generative AI** - AI evolution, transformers, attention, tokenization
2. **The LLM Training Lifecycle** - Pre-training, RLHF, fine-tuning, LoRA
3. **Prompt Engineering and Context Engineering** - Prompting techniques, context assembly
4. **Prompt Management at Scale** - Version control, testing, templates for production prompts
5. **LLMs and the AI Ecosystem** - Model comparison, providers, frameworks
6. **RAG Fundamentals and Document Ingestion** - RAG architecture, parsing, chunking
7. **Embeddings, Vector Databases, and Retrieval** - Embeddings, similarity search, hybrid retrieval
